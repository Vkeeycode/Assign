Description

Vkeey Studio was founded in 2022 with the primary focus of providing world class photography services to all our numerous clients within Port Harcourt and it's environment. We specialize in digital imagery but not limited to portrait, fashion, commercial,product and events photography. Our team consist of photographers with passion and skills for various genre’s of photography and other professionals including retouchers, graphics designers and web designers.

Vkeey Studio offers a unique solution for your creative needs Whether it’s documenting your wedding day, shooting your engagement photos, or capturing your corporate vision; there is a story that’s dying to be told and we want to help you tell that story. As a creative studio, we don’t have a routine for every session we work with you to decide what works best for your specific needs We interact with our client to get an understanding of there imagination and the kind of images they want us to create. Our goal is to provide you with the most beautiful version of your day, your moment, your life.
